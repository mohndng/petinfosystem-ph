About PetInfoSys

A comprehensive digital solution for Barangay Animal Registry and Public Health Management.

Objective

To digitalize and centralize animal records at the barangay level, ensuring accurate population tracking, efficient vaccination monitoring, and rapid response to bite incidents.

Purpose

To support the implementation of RA 9482 (Anti-Rabies Act) by providing LGUs with modern tools to manage pet data, improve public safety, and promote responsible pet ownership.

Key Features

Digital Registry

Centralized database for pet and owner profiles with secure cloud storage.

Vaccination Tracking

Automated monitoring of anti-rabies and core vaccinations with reminders.

Incident Reporting

Management system for bite incidents, observation periods, and stray reports.

AI & Analytics

Smart executive summaries and visual charts for data-driven decisions.

Public Portal

Dedicated access for residents to verify records and view announcements.

Certificates

Instant PDF generation for registration certificates with branding options.
